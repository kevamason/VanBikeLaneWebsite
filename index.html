Bike Lane Debate

Vancouver, BC

In 2009, the City of Vancouver reallocated one vehicle travel lane on Burrard Bridge (the busiest connection in the bike network) to provide separated bicycle lanes into and out of the downtown core. Since then, separated bike lanes have been a controversial issue with an overwhelming supply of resources dedicated to supporting for and against arguments.

It is difficult for residents of Vancouver to find an unbiased collection of educational material on the controversial topic. Our goal is to provide residents of Vancouver with a neutral, trustworthy source of educational material on the bike lane issue through a user-friendly website.
